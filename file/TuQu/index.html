IEEE pronounced “I-Triple-E” is the world’s largest professional association with its corporate office in New York. It was formed in 1963 from the amalgamation of Engineers. Today, IEEE have more than 4 million members and Student Branches in nearly seven thousand colleges and universities all around the world...

MEA IEEE Student Branch was established in year 2011. From the time of establishment onwards the Student Branch has been actively organizing and participating events all around the Kerala Section. There are societies like Industrial Application Society and Computer Society under IEEE. And Affinity Group likes Women in Engineering that motivates girl students.

MEA IEEE Student Branch have members from all the departments (CSE, IT, ECE, EEE, ME, CE).
